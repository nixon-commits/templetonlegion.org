About Post 220

The American Legion Post 220 has been serving veterans and the Templeton community for over 100 years. We are committed to supporting veterans, active duty personnel, and their families while giving back to our community.

Whether you're looking for camaraderie, ways to give back, or to honor your service, Post 220 is a place for you. Be part of something bigger and contribute to the legacy of those who have served. Your involvement makes a difference!

Membership Eligibility

To be eligible for membership in The American Legion, you must have served on federal active duty in the United States Armed Forces and have been honorably discharged, or are still serving on active duty.
